Potatoes

Nutrition

Potatoes provide a great source of nutrition. They contain almost everything the human body needs to sustain itself. They are a good source of vitamins C and B6, manganese, phosphorus, niacin and pantothenic acid. (ref.)

Did you know?

In the movie Martian, Matt Damon survived alone on Mars for a bajillion years by growing and eating potatoes.

Energy

Potatoes don't just provide energy to human bodies. They can also provide energy to lightbulbs! Trying plugging a zinc screw and a copper penny into a potato, and connecting a light-emitting diode between them. It glows! That shit glows, dammit! Potatoes are awesome.

Did you know?

This works on oranges, lemons, and other objects that contain an "electrolytic fluid" in them.
